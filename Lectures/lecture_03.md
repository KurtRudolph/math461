\[ (1 + x)^n = \sum\limits_{k=0}^n {n \choose k} x^k \]
\[ {n \choose k} = \frac{ n!}{ k!(n-k)!} \]

NOTE: \[k!\] is the number of permutation of \[k\] objects.

### ex

\[ (1+x)^4 = (1 + x)(1 + x)(1 + x)(1 + x) = 1 + 4x + 6x^2 + 4x^3 + x^3\]
* \[6x^2 = {4 \choose 2}\]
