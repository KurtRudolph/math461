### Binomial Theorem
\[{\left( {1 + x} \right)^h} = \sum\limits_{k = 0}^n {n \choose k} x^k \]

\[ {n \choose k} = \frac{ n!}{ k!( n - k)!}\]

\[{\left( {1 + x} \right)^h} = (1 + x)(1 + 3x + 3x^2 + x^3)\]
