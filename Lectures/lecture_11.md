## DNHI 

Show that \[\sum\limits_{n = 1}^{\infty}{ \frac{ 1}{ n(n + 1)} = 1\]

* \[\sum\limits_{n = 1}^{\infty}{ \frac{ 1}{ n(n + 1)} = \sum\limits_{n = 1}^{\infty}{ \left(\frac{ 1}{ n} = \frac{ 1}{ n+1}\right) = 1\]
  * telescoping series
